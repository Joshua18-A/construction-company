Synthesis of company's profile

Building Audit Systems Automation Limited (BASA Limited) is a design-and-build consultancy company incorporated by the corporate Affairs Commission,Abuja in February 28 2012,under the companies and Allied Matters Act of 1990

BASA Limited commenced operations under this name in February 2017.However,our management team have handled projects elsewhere for over twenty years. simple list of project is attached for perusal.

Our core business is providing services,through a network on in house and outsourced professionals in the fields of Architecture,Building Construction,Projects Development,Facilities Management and Arts.Our subsidiaries cater to services like Interior Design,Mechanical and Electrical Engineering,Landscape,FurnitureDesign.Our thrust is to provide cutting edge services to meet the client's needs.
